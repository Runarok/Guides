## ⚔️ *The 48 Laws of Power — Original & Mine*

---

### **1. Never Outshine the Master**

* **Original**: Always make superiors feel superior.
* **Mine**: I move in silence. I let others feel celebrated while I secure the real gains. I adapt the method so they see the praise, not the play.

---

### **2. Never Put Too Much Trust in Friends, Learn How to Use Enemies**

* **Original**: Friends are emotional; enemies are strategic.
* **Mine**: I give value, but never hold too tight. I keep friends close, enemies closer — and sweeten my presence just enough that even rivals hesitate to unite against me. I let everyone think they’re winning while I’m positioning.

---

### **3. Conceal Your Intentions**

* **Original**: Clarity invites sabotage.
* **Mine**: I give just enough to distract, never enough to decode. I play the red herring card. My silence is the smoke — my real moves are always deeper.

---

### **4. Always Say Less Than Necessary**

* **Original**: Power thrives in silence and mystery.
* **Mine**: I speak only where words can’t be twisted. If someone turns everything into a weapon, I disappear from their reach. My silence isn't fear — it's precision.

---

### **5. So Much Depends on Reputation — Guard It With Your Life**

* **Original**: Reputation is armor and weapon.
* **Mine**: Be so useful, so consistent, that your flaws get ignored. Stay human, but make your value undeniable — no one betrays what they still benefit from.

---

### **6. Court Attention at All Costs**

* **Original**: Obscurity kills influence.
* **Mine**: Let them know you help, not how much you hold. Show warmth, not your core. Stay the person they respect even if they don’t fully understand.

---

### **7. Get Others to Do the Work for You, but Always Take the Credit**

* **Original**: Results matter more than effort.
* **Mine**: I fix what’s easy for me but hard for them — without showing how little it cost. They feel rescued, not outshone. Power isn’t in showing off; it’s in knowing when not to.
