**Procedure:**



* Open **MATLAB** → **App** → **Antenna Designer**

* Select geometry → **Yagi-Uda** or **Dipole**

* Set frequency → **Yagi-Uda: 2.652 GHz**, **Dipole: 4 MHz**

* Click **Close/Accept** to confirm the frequency

* Get **Impedance**, **S-Parameter**, **Current Distribution**, and **Pattern (Azimuth/Elevation)** by clicking the respective analysis buttons

* Click **Export** → **Export as Script** to generate a MATLAB script for the design
